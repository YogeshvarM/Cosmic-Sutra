Legend & Basics

🌟 Four Pillars

  • Grahas: The nine planets (Navagrahas) are the main actors influencing life's areas.
  • Rāśis: The twelve zodiac signs — the field through which planets act, each with its own nature and element.
  • Bhāvas: The twelve houses represent areas of life — Self, Wealth, Communication, etc.
  • Vargas: Divisional charts (D-charts) give finer details — D-9 (Navāṁśa) for marriage/dharma, D-10 for profession, etc.

🏛️ House Groups

  • Kendra (1, 4, 7, 10): "Pillars" of life — foundation of personal, domestic, social, and professional existence.
  • Trikona (1, 5, 9): Houses of Dharma — luck, intelligence, and life purpose.
  • Upacchaya (3, 6, 10, 11): Growth houses — areas that improve through effort and time.
  • Dusthāna (6, 8, 12): Difficult or transformative houses — challenges, debts, disease, and spiritual release.

⚡ Digbala (Directional Strength)

Each planet gains maximum strength in certain directions:

  • Sun & Mars (10th House): Power and visibility (southern direction).
  • Jupiter & Mercury (1st House): Wisdom and intellect (eastern direction).
  • Moon & Venus (4th House): Peace and emotional contentment (northern direction).
  • Saturn (7th House): Endurance and worldly discipline (western direction).

⚠️ MKS (Marana Kāraka Sthāna)

The "death-like" position for a planet — where its significations weaken.

  • Sun (12th): Loss of vitality, obscured ego.
  • Moon (8th): Emotional instability, hidden mind.
  • Mars (7th): Misplaced aggression.
  • Mercury (7th): Distracted intellect.
  • Jupiter (3rd): Diminished wisdom.
  • Venus (6th): Struggles in pleasure/relationships.
  • Saturn (1st): Weak vitality or self-confidence.
  • Rahu (9th): Unsteady faith.
  • Ketu (4th): Emotional detachment.

🔑 Natural Karakas (Significators)

Each planet naturally represents life areas:

  • Sun: Soul, father, authority
  • Moon: Mind, mother, emotions
  • Mars: Courage, energy, siblings
  • Mercury: Intellect, communication
  • Jupiter: Wisdom, children, teacher
  • Venus: Relationships, comforts
  • Saturn: Work, endurance, longevity
  • Rahu/Ketu: Material obsession / spiritual release

☀️ Ādityas (Solar Deities)

The twelve Ādityas are divine aspects of the Sun that preside over each zodiac sign — they grant their unique virtues:

  • Aries – Indra: Leadership, initiative, courage
  • Taurus – Ravi: Stability, strength
  • Gemini – Aryaman: Communication, kinship
  • Cancer – Vishnu: Preservation, emotional wisdom
  • Leo – Mitra: Friendship, generosity
  • Virgo – Aryama: Service, duty, refinement
  • Libra – Vivasvan: Balance, fairness, social grace
  • Scorpio – Twashtha: Transformation, regeneration
  • Sagittarius – Bhaga: Fortune, divine joy
  • Capricorn – Pushan: Guidance, protection
  • Aquarius – Varuna: Cosmic order, morality
  • Pisces – Mitra/Vishnu: Compassion, spiritual union
